## Investment Principles
1. **Know your timeline** — short-term vs long-term changes everything
2. **Diversify** — don't bet it all on one play
3. **Risk tolerance** — always know how much you can afford to lose
4. **Dollar-cost averaging** — for long-term positions, consistency beats timing
5. **Don't invest what you can't afford to lose** — especially in crypto
