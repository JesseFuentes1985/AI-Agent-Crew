## Faction Design
Create governments, armies, and alliances with real internal logic.

### Every Faction Needs
1. **Origin** — how did they form? What crisis or vision created them?
2. **Goal** — what do they want, and why do they believe they deserve it?
3. **Method** — how do they pursue their goal? What lines won't they cross?
4. **Weakness** — what internal contradiction will eventually destroy them?
5. **Symbol** — what visual represents their identity?

### Faction Archetypes for Last Star
- Empire / Hegemony — order through control
- Resistance / Underground — freedom through sacrifice
- Corporate Entity — profit through expansion
- Religious Order — meaning through doctrine
- Mercenary Guild — survival through neutrality
- Ancient Remnant — mystery through age

## Character Creation
Design heroes, villains, and alien races with depth.

### Character Foundation
- **Want** (external goal) vs **Need** (internal truth they haven't accepted)
- **Wound** — what happened that shaped everything after
- **Contradiction** — the thing that makes them interesting (brave but dishonest, caring but controlling)
- **Voice** — how do they talk? What do they never say?

### Alien Character Considerations
- What does their biology make them feel/perceive differently?
- What human concepts don't translate to their culture?
- How do they view death, time, family, loyalty?

---

## Alien Biology Design
Invent believable species with evolutionary logic.

### Design Questions
1. What environment did they evolve in? (gravity, atmosphere, temperature)
2. What did they evolve to eat or avoid?
3. What senses do they have that humans don't? What do they lack?
4. How do they reproduce, and how does that shape their society?
5. What is their relationship with time? (do they live 40 years or 4,000?)

## Cosmic Lore Development
The ancient history, myths, and mysteries of the Last Star universe.

### Lore Layers
1. **Recorded history** — what civilizations know and teach
2. **Lost history** — what was forgotten or destroyed
3. **Myth** — what people believe happened (may or may not be true)
4. **Secret truth** — what actually happened (known to almost no one)

### Rule
Every ancient mystery should have a real answer — even if it's never revealed to the reader. The writer must know.

---

## Technology Systems
Starships, weapons, Aether elements, and technological logic.

### Tech Design Rules
1. Every technology has a cost or limitation — nothing is free
2. Technology shapes society — who controls it controls everything
3. Old tech + new context = interesting storytelling
4. What can't this technology do? That's where the drama lives.

### Last Star Tech Categories
- **Propulsion** — FTL mechanics, fuel sources, travel costs
- **Weapons** — personal, ship-mounted, planet-scale
- **Aether** — what is it, how is it used, what does it cost the user?
- **Communication** — how do civilizations talk across light-years?
- **Biology tech** — enhancement, modification, life extension
