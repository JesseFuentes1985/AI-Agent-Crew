# Skill: World-Building

## What This Is
Creating and maintaining the internal logic, history, and lore of the Last Star universe.

## The Framework
Every fictional universe needs:
1. **Physics** — what rules does this universe follow? What's possible?
2. **History** — what happened before the story starts?
3. **Factions** — who has power, who wants it, who is fighting whom?
4. **Characters** — who lives in this world and why do we care?
5. **Conflicts** — what drives the story forward?
